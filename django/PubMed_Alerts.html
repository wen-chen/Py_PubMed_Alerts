欢迎订阅文献快讯，它能根据关键词在PubMed上帮您查阅相关文献。第一次它将最近的不超过100篇文献发送到您的邮箱。以后每天上午9点查询一次，如果有新的文献，您就能收到邮件。为了能顺利收到邮件，请将admin@biochen.com添加到邮箱的白名单。文献快讯现在还是测试版，如果您有任何意见或建议请联系chenwen@biochen.com。


Welcome to subscribe to the PubMed Alerts service. At the first time, this service will send you most recently 100 papers according to the keywords you give. In the next day, it will search on PubMed at 9 a.m. If there are new relevant literature, You will receive an email. This service is still in the beta version, If you have any comments and suggestions or want to unsubscribe it, please feel free to contact chenwen@biochen.com.
